  • Size of the Land Comparison
  • Condition of House Assessment
  • Size of House Comparison
  • Design and Layout Evaluation

Deal-killers

+
  • Main Roads Proximity
  • Housing Commission > 20%
  • Big Renovations Required
  • Backing onto Rail Tracks
  • Powerlines Nearby
  • Proximity to Industrial Areas
  • Cemeteries Nearby
  • Weird or Odd Size Blocks
  • Multiple Extremely Small Bedrooms
  • Bus Stop Outside House
  • Busy Road Adjacency
  • Roundabout Proximity
  • Directly Facing Schools
  • Highways Nearby
  • Energy Substations
  • Major or Unapproved Renovations
  • Swimming Pools (maintenance consideration)
  • Land Size Assessment (Land size under 375m²)

Questions I ask the agent

+
  • Are you entertaining offers before auction?
  • Do you have any listings where the vendor didn't want to pay VPA (Vendor Paid advertising)?
  • To find out the reserve, go with a range (is it above, is it below?)
  • Will anything come up with the Building and Pest?
  • What's the payout figure on the loan?
  • What is the lowest they are willing to accept?
  • What is the sales process? (e.g. blind auction for regional markets)

Things I've learnt I could apply to Alaya Property

Potential sources of clients

+
  • Explore partnerships with mortgage brokers, financial advisers and sales agents to get warm leads
  • Sub-contract / equity sharing if don't have licenses in specific markets
  • Look at communities like HTAG and other forums (e.g. Aus Property Forums) for people looking for buyers agents

Sales Agents relationship building

+
  • Elevator pitch for high volume agents: I'm a buyers agent looking to do volume, I have preapproved buyers and I'm looking for deals, can I take you out for a coffee?
  • Be positive and come in to solve a problem

Getting deals done

+
  • Get deals done before midweek before Saturday opens (secure before the open home)
  • Try and secure a price reduction based on the Building and Pest inspection, but you want to get the deal done
  • Put in multiple offers and see if one of them sticks

Alternative ways to source off-market

+
  • Call property managers to see if they have any potential off markets
  • Potentially explore forums, do they have anyone selling?
  • Facebook advertising / exploring marketplace
